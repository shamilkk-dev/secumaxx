## 5. Distributor Business Plan

### 5.1 Objectives
1. Expand authorised distribution reach across Kerala (starting hubs: Kozhikode & Ernakulam).
2. Build a one-stop-shop model covering surveillance, access control, networking, fire safety, and entrance automation.
3. Strengthen after-sales and installation support as a competitive differentiator.
4. Grow project sales (B2B/institutional) alongside retail/dealer sales.

### 5.2 Target Customer Segments
- System integrators & installers (dealer network)
- Corporate offices & IT parks (access control, networking, PA systems)
- Hospitality (hotel locks, safe locks, CCTV)
- Retail chains (AM/anti-theft systems, CCTV, entrance control)
- Government & institutional projects (fire safety, entrance control, baggage scanners, metal detectors)
- Residential complexes/gated communities (intrusion alarms, video door phones, fire alarms)

### 5.4 Phased Rollout Plan

**Phase 1 – Foundation (Months 1–2)**
- Finalize brand agreements/stock levels for core categories (CCTV, access control, networking).
- Set up dealer pricing sheets by category and brand.
- Train sales teams at Kozhikode & Ernakulam branches on full 20-category portfolio.

**Phase 2 – Market Activation (Months 3–4)**
- Launch dealer recruitment drive in Calicut and Kochi regions.
- Run product demo days for high-margin lines: Ajax fire & alarm systems, eSSL entrance control, Netgear AV.
- Publish product portfolio & pricing collateral (leverage existing portfolio brochure) to dealer network and via social channels.

**Phase 3 – Project Pipeline Development (Months 5–6)**
- Target institutional projects: fire & life safety (EN 54 certified), entrance control (barriers/turnstiles), baggage scanners, metal detectors for corporate/government clients.
- Assign dedicated project sales follow-through (9886042300) with pre-sales consultation support.

**Phase 4 – Scale & Retain (Months 7–12)**
- Expand structured cabling and PA system offerings bundled with CCTV/access control projects.
- Formalize after-sale service SLAs (AMC contracts) for installed systems.
- Review branch performance (Kozhikode vs Ernakulam) and evaluate opening a third hub based on demand.

### 5.5 Key Success Metrics
- Number of active dealers per branch
- Project sales pipeline value & conversion rate
- Category-wise revenue mix (CCTV vs Access Control vs Fire Safety, etc.)
- After-sales/AMC contract renewal rate
- Brand-wise sell-through (Hikvision, Ajax, eSSL, Matrix, Netgear)

### 5.6 Risks & Mitigation
| Risk | Mitigation |
|------|------------|
| Heavy reliance on a few brands | Diversify with multi-brand options already in portfolio (e.g., Hikvision + Ajax + Matrix for CCTV/alarms) |
| Regional concentration (Kerala only) | Use project sales team to explore adjacent state institutional tenders |
| Installation/service bottlenecks | Build certified installer/dealer network with training support |
| Payment delays from project clients | Centralize billing/payment tracking via dedicated contact (9497340008) |
